**Return to real life** with a chat app designed for planning social events.

## The big idea

In a sea of chat apps, **Planet** is designed to help users plan social events with friends and return to real life.

Users can:
- Create a group with friends
- Schedule events

For a group or event, users can:
- Start a chat with group members
- Assign tasks to members
- Create polls
